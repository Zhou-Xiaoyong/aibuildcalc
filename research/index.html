About our research

AIBuildCalc Research produces independent, data-driven reports on home improvement and construction economics. Our methodology combines federal datasets (U.S. Census Bureau, Bureau of Labor Statistics), industry cost indexes (RSMeans), trade-association standards (NRCA, ARMA, ICC), and aggregated contractor pricing surveys.

We do not accept payment from manufacturers or contractors for inclusion in our reports. All figures are estimates for planning purposes and should be verified with licensed local professionals. Reports are free to cite with attribution.

Have a research topic suggestion or a correction? We welcome feedback from contractors, academics, and industry professionals.
