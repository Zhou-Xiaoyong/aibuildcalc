Expert Insights Smart analysis for your Electrical project
⚡
80% Load Rule Enforcement
NEC requires circuits loaded to no more than 80% of breaker rating for continuous loads. AI enforces this automatically in all calculations.
🔌
GFCI/AFCI Requirements
GFCI: kitchens, baths, garages, outdoors. AFCI: bedrooms, living areas (2023 NEC). AI flags required protection for your circuit type.
📉
Voltage Drop Optimization
Over 3% drop causes motor overheating. AI recommends wire gauge upgrades when your run length would cause excessive voltage drop.

Calculation Formula

Frequently Asked Questions

What wire gauge for 20 amp circuit?
12 AWG copper for 20 amp circuits up to 100 ft. Step up to 10 AWG for longer runs.
What happens if wire gauge is too small?
Undersized wire causes overheating, voltage drop, and fire hazard.
