How to Use This Calculator

Accurately calculating shingle quantities prevents delays and waste. Our calculator accounts for roof area, shingle type, and waste factor.

  1. Measure or calculate your total roof area
  2. Select your shingle type
  3. Choose waste factor
  4. Enter specifications in the calculator

Frequently Asked Questions

How many shingle bundles per square?
3-tab: 3 bundles per square. Architectural: 3.5 bundles. Premium: 4 bundles per square.
What waste factor should I use?
Use 10% for simple gable roofs, 15% for hip roofs, and 20% for complex roofs with multiple dormers and valleys.
