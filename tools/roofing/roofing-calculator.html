How to Use This Calculator

Planning a roofing project? Accurate material estimates prevent costly over-ordering. Our calculator accounts for roof pitch to give you true surface area.

  1. Measure the length and width of your roof at the base
  2. Determine your roof pitch
  3. Enter dimensions in the calculator
  4. Add 10-15% for waste, valleys, and ridges

Pitch-to-Cost Analysis

Frequently Asked Questions

How many bundles of shingles do I need?
You need 3 bundles of standard 3-tab shingles per roofing square (100 sq ft). For architectural shingles, you may need 4 bundles per square.
What is a roofing square?
A roofing square equals 100 square feet of roof area, the standard unit for measuring and pricing.
How much does a new roof cost?
Average roof replacement costs $5,000-$12,000 for asphalt shingles, $12,000-$25,000 for metal, and $20,000+ for slate or tile.
