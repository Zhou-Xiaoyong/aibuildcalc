How to Use This Calculator

Roof sheathing supports shingles and transfers loads to the framing. Our calculator estimates sheets, cost, and fasteners needed.

  1. Calculate total roof area
  2. Select sheathing sheet size
  3. Choose thickness based on rafter spacing
  4. Add 10% waste
Expert Insights Smart analysis for your Roofing project
🏠
Pitch-to-Cost Analysis
Steep roofs (8/12+) need 20-40% more material and higher labor costs. Our AI adjusts estimates based on your exact pitch ratio.
📐
Waste Factor Intelligence
Gable roofs: 10-15% waste. Hip roofs: 15-20%. Complex roofs with dormers: 20-25%. AI selects the right factor for your roof type.
🌧️
Climate Zone Recommendations
Ice & water shield required in northern zones. AI checks your region's IRC climate zone for code-compliant underlayment suggestions.

Calculation Formula

Sheets = Roof area / Sheet area + 10% waste Fasteners = Sheets x 60 (8d nails per sheet)

Frequently Asked Questions

What thickness roof sheathing do I need?
1/2" OSB or plywood is standard for 24" truss/rafter spacing. Use 5/8" for 32" spacing or heavy snow loads.
