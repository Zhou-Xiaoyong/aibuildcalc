Calculation Formula

Rafter length = (Span / 2) / cos(arctan(pitch/12)) Plumb cut angle = arctan(pitch/12) x (180/pi) Rafters = (Ridge length / Spacing) + 1

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate rafter length?
For a 6/12 pitch on a 24 ft span: rafter run = 12 ft, rise = 6 ft, rafter length = sqrt(12^2 + 6^2) = 13.42 ft per side.
What size lumber for rafters?
2x6 for spans up to 10 ft, 2x8 for 10-15 ft, 2x10 for 15-20 ft, 2x12 for 20+ ft. Check IRC span tables.
