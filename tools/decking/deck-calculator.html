How to Use This Calculator

A well-planned deck starts with accurate material estimates. Our calculator helps you determine boards, joists, and footings.

  1. Measure deck length and width
  2. Choose decking material
  3. Select joist spacing
  4. Enter specifications
  5. Add 10-15% waste
AI Insights Smart analysis for your Decking project

Wood vs Composite TCO
Pressure-treated: $15-25/sqft, lasts 15 years. Composite: $30-60/sqft, lasts 25-50 years with zero annual sealing. AI compares true lifetime cost.
📏
Joist Spacing Intelligence
16" OC for wood decking, 12" OC required for most composite. AI checks your material choice and adjusts joist count automatically.
⚠️
Ledger Board Safety
#1 deck failure source: improper house attachment. AI flags critical flashing and lag bolt requirements for code-compliant installation.

Calculation Formula

Area = L x W Boards = ((W x 12) / Board width) x (L + 1) Joists = (L x 12 / Joist spacing) + 1

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a deck cost?
Pressure-treated wood: $15-$30/sq ft. Composite: $30-$45/sq ft. A 16x12 deck costs $2,880-$7,200.
What spacing should deck joists be?
Standard: 16" OC for wood. 12" OC for composite. 24" only for 2x6 boards.
