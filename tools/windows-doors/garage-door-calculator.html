How to Use This Calculator

Replacing or installing a garage door? Our calculator figures door area, a cost estimate, and the vertical track length you'll need based on the opening and door type.

  1. Measure the opening width and height
  2. Select the garage door type
  3. Enter values in the calculator
  4. Verify headroom clearance for tracks and springs

Calculation Formula

Door area = Opening width x Opening height Estimated cost = ceil(Door area x $35/sq ft) Track length = Opening height x 2 + 3 ft (Type multipliers: Sectional 1.0, Roll-up 1.3, Tilt 0.9)

Frequently Asked Questions

What are standard garage door sizes?
Standard single garage doors are 8 or 9 feet wide by 7 feet tall. Double doors are 16 feet wide by 7 feet tall. Custom heights of 8 feet are common for trucks and RVs.
How much does a garage door cost installed?
A standard steel sectional garage door costs $700-$1,500 installed. Insulated doors run $1,000-$2,500, and custom wood doors can exceed $3,000-$5,000.
How much headroom is needed for a garage door?
Sectional doors need at least 12 inches of headroom above the opening for tracks and springs. Roll-up doors need as little as 6-8 inches, making them ideal for low clearance.
