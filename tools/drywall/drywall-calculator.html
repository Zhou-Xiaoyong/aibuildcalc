Calculation Formula

Frequently Asked Questions

How many drywall sheets for 12x12 room?
About 16 sheets of 4x8 drywall (10 walls + 6 ceiling). Add 10% waste.
Should I use 1/2" or 5/8" drywall?
1/2" for walls with 16" stud spacing. 5/8" for ceilings (prevents sagging) and fire-rated walls.
