How to Use This Calculator

Our calculator estimates posts, top rail, chain link fabric, and hardware for your project.

  1. Measure total fence line length
  2. Choose fence height
  3. Determine post spacing
  4. Enter specifications
  5. Order gate hardware separately
AI Insights Smart analysis for your Fencing project
📐
Post Depth by Climate
Posts need 2ft minimum depth, or below frost line (36-48" in northern states). AI checks your region's frost depth for stable post settings.
🔩
Hardware Longevity Guide
Galvanized hardware lasts 15-20 years. Vinyl-coated: 20-25. Stainless steel: 25+. AI recommends based on coastal proximity and humidity.
📋
Setback & Permit Check
Most municipalities require fences 2-8" inside property lines. AI reminds you to verify local codes and HOA rules before ordering materials.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does chain link fence cost?
$8-$20 per linear foot installed (4ft height). A 100ft fence costs $800-$3,000.
How far apart should chain link posts be?
Standard spacing is 10 ft on center for residential chain link.
