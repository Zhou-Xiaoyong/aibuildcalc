How to Use This Calculator

Our calculator adds floor, wall, and shower areas together and accounts for higher waste in wet areas.

  1. Measure bathroom floor dimensions
  2. Estimate shower wall area
  3. Select tile size
  4. Enter specifications
  5. Use 15% waste for bathrooms

Calculation Formula

Floor area = Length x Width Total area = Floor + Shower walls Tiles = (Total / Tile area) x 1.15 (15% waste)

Frequently Asked Questions

How much tile for a bathroom?
A typical 5x8 bathroom needs 40 sq ft floor + 50-60 sq ft shower = 90-100 sq ft total. With 15% waste, order about 115 sq ft.
What tile is best for bathroom floors?
Porcelain tile is best - water-resistant, durable, available in many styles. Avoid polished ceramic (slippery when wet).
