Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a kitchen backsplash cost?
Subway tile: $7-$15/sq ft installed. Glass mosaic: $20-$40/sq ft. A typical 20 sq ft backsplash costs $140-$800 installed.
How high should a kitchen backsplash be?
Standard: 18 inches (between counter and upper cabinets). Full-height backsplashes are becoming more popular.
