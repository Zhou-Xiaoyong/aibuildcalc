How to Use This Calculator

Our calculator estimates square footage and total cost based on counter dimensions and material choice.

  1. Measure total length of all counter runs
  2. Use standard 25.5" depth
  3. Select countertop material
  4. Enter specifications
  5. Factor in sink cutouts (deduct 3-5 sq ft each)

Countertop Material Guide
Granite: $40-100/sqft, needs yearly sealing. Quartz: $50-120/sqft, zero maintenance. AI compares 10-year cost including maintenance.
💧
Waterproofing Requirements
Tile and grout are NOT waterproof. AI flags the need for waterproof membrane behind shower walls — the #1 source of shower failures.
📏
Standard Dimension Guide
Counter height: 36". Overhang: 1-1.5". Backsplash: 18-20". AI validates your measurements against standard dimensions.

Calculation Formula

Area = Length x (Depth / 12) Slabs = Area / 45 (standard slab ~45 sq ft) Cost = Area x Price per sq ft

Frequently Asked Questions

How much do quartz countertops cost?
$50-$120 per sq ft installed. A typical 30 sq ft kitchen costs $1,500-$3,600 for quartz.
Is granite or quartz better?
Quartz is non-porous and more durable. Granite is natural stone with unique patterns but requires annual sealing.
