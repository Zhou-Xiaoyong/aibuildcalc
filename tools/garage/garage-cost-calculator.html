How to Use This Calculator

Adding a garage boosts home value and protects your vehicles. This calculator estimates total cost based on footprint, whether it's attached or detached, and the finish tier you choose.

  1. Enter the length and width of your planned garage
  2. Choose Attached or Detached (detached costs more per sq ft)
  3. Pick a finish level: Basic, Standard, or Premium
  4. Review total cost and cost per square foot
Expert Insights Smart analysis for your Garage project
🏠
Attached vs Detached
Attached garages run $40/sq ft thanks to a shared wall and foundation. Detached garages average $55/sq ft with four independent walls and a separate slab.
🎨
Finish Tier Pricing
Basic is the raw shell. Standard adds insulation and drywall (1.15x). Premium includes epoxy floors, cabinets, and finished walls (1.4x).
📐
Standard Garage Sizes
1-car: 12x24 ft (288 sq ft). 2-car: 24x24 ft (576 sq ft). 3-car: 36x24 ft (864 sq ft). Deeper garages add storage bays.

Calculation Formula

Area = Length x Width Base cost/sq ft = Detached ? $55 : $40 Multiplier = Premium ? 1.4 : Standard ? 1.15 : 1 Estimated cost = ceil(Area x Base cost/sq ft x Multiplier) Cost per sq ft = ceil(Estimated cost / Area)

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to build a garage?
A standard 2-car garage (24x24 ft) costs $23,000-$32,000 attached or $32,000-$44,000 detached, depending on finish level. Cost runs $40-$55 per square foot before premium upgrades.
Is an attached or detached garage cheaper?
Attached garages cost about $40 per sq ft because they share a wall and foundation with the house. Detached garages run around $55 per sq ft since they need four walls and a separate foundation.
How many square feet is a 2-car garage?
A standard 2-car garage is 24x24 ft, or 576 square feet. A 1-car garage is typically 12x24 ft (288 sq ft), and a 3-car garage is 36x24 ft (864 sq ft).
