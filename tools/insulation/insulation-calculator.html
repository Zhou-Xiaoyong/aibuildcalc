Frequently Asked Questions

What R-value do I need for my attic?
R-38 to R-49 for most US climate zones (Zones 4-8). Southern zones (1-3): R-30 to R-38.
How much does attic insulation cost?
DIY fiberglass batts: $0.30-$1.50/sq ft. Professional blown-in: $1.00-$2.50/sq ft.
Can I add new insulation over old?
Yes. Use unfaced batts or blown-in over existing. Do not compress old insulation.
