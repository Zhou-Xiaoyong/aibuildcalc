Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to paint a room professionally?
Professional painting costs $1.50-$3.50 per square foot. A standard 12x10 room costs $380-$790.
Is it cheaper to paint yourself?
DIY costs $100-$250 for a 12x10 room. That is 50-70% less than hiring a pro.
