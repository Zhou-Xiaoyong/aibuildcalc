Frequently Asked Questions

How many pallets of sod do I need?
One standard pallet covers 450 sq ft. A 50x30 ft lawn (1,500 sq ft) needs about 3.5 pallets with 5% waste.
How much does sod cost?
$0.30-$0.80 per sq ft or $130-$380 per pallet. Professional installation adds $0.50-$1.00 per sq ft.
