Mulch Depth by Purpose
Weed suppression: 3-4". Moisture retention: 2-3". Decorative: 1-2". AI recommends depth based on your project goals.

Gravel Base Requirements
Driveways: 4-6" compacted base + 2-3" gravel. Paths: 2-3" base + 2" gravel. AI adjusts for vehicle vs foot traffic loads.

Calculation Formula

Volume (cu ft) = Length x Width x (Depth / 12) Cubic yards = Volume / 27 Tons = Cubic yards x Density factor (1.4-1.7)

Frequently Asked Questions

How much gravel for a driveway?
4-6 inches depth. A 12x50 ft driveway at 4" deep needs about 7.4 cu yd or 10 tons.
How much does gravel cost?
Bulk gravel: $15-$75 per cubic yard or $20-$100 per ton depending on type. Delivery adds $50-$100.
