Frequently Asked Questions

How much mulch do I need?
One cubic yard covers 324 sq ft at 1" deep, 162 sq ft at 2", or 108 sq ft at 3".
Is bulk mulch cheaper than bagged?
Bulk ($20-$45/cu yd) is significantly cheaper than bagged ($50-$80/cu yd equivalent). Buy bags only for small areas.
How deep should mulch be?
2-3 inches for existing beds, 3-4 for new beds. Never exceed 4 inches.
