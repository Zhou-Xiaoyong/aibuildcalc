Frequently Asked Questions

How many pavers for a 12x12 patio?
144 sq ft. With 4x8 pavers (0.22 sq ft each), need 654 pavers + 10% = 720 pavers.
How much does a paver patio cost?
DIY: $8-$15/sq ft. Professional: $15-$30/sq ft. A 200 sq ft patio costs $1,600-$6,000.
