How to Use This Calculator

Our siding calculator accounts for wall dimensions, openings, and waste to give you a precise material estimate.

  1. Measure perimeter of exterior walls
  2. Determine average wall height
  3. Select siding material
  4. Enter specifications
  5. Subtract window and door areas
AI Insights Smart analysis for your Siding project
🏠
Vinyl vs Fiber Cement TCO
Vinyl: $3-7/sqft installed, 20-40 year life. Fiber cement: $6-13/sqft, 50+ years. AI calculates 30-year total cost of ownership.
💧
House Wrap & Flashing
Proper WRB and flashing prevents 90% of water damage. AI includes house wrap square footage in material estimates.
🌡️
Thermal Expansion Allowance
Vinyl siding needs 1/4" gap at overlaps for expansion. AI calculates total material needed accounting for expansion gaps.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does siding cost?
Vinyl: $5-$8/sq ft. Wood: $7-$14/sq ft. Fiber cement: $6-$12/sq ft. A 1,500 sq ft home costs $7,500-$18,000 for vinyl.
What is a square of siding?
A square equals 100 square feet of siding, the standard unit for ordering and pricing.
