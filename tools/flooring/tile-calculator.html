Frequently Asked Questions

How much tile for a 12x12 room?
144 sq ft. With 12x12 tiles, need 144 + 10% waste = 158 tiles. About 16 boxes.
What waste factor for tile?
Use 10% for straight layouts, 15% for diagonal, 20% for intricate patterns.
