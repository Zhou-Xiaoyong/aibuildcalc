How to Use This Calculator

Carpet is sold by square yard. Our calculator converts room dimensions and estimates material and installation costs.

  1. Measure room length and width
  2. Select carpet quality
  3. Enter measurements
  4. Add 10% for pattern matching

Pattern Waste Calculator
Straight lay: 10% waste. Diagonal: 15%. Herringbone: 20%. AI selects the right waste factor for your pattern — not a flat percentage.
📏
Subfloor Readiness Check
80% of flooring failures trace to subfloor issues. AI flags moisture, leveling, and prep requirements based on your material choice.

Material Lifespan Comparison
Hardwood: 100+ years (refinishable). Laminate: 15-25 years. Vinyl plank: 20-30 years. AI calculates cost-per-year for each option.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much carpet for 12x14 room?
168 sq ft or about 18.7 sq yd. With 10% waste, order 21 sq yd.
How much does carpet cost installed?
Budget: $2-$4/sq ft. Standard: $4-$7/sq ft. Premium: $7-$12/sq ft installed.
