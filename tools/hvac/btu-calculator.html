Frequently Asked Questions

How many BTU per square foot?
Cooling: 20-25 BTU/sq ft. Heating: 30-40 BTU/sq ft. Kitchens and sunrooms need 10-20% more.
What does AC tonnage mean?
One ton = 12,000 BTU/h. A 2-ton unit provides 24,000 BTU/h. Most residential AC units range 1.5-5 tons.
What happens if my AC is oversized?
Oversized units short-cycle, reducing dehumidification, increasing energy bills, and shortening equipment life.
