How to Use This Calculator

Our calculator estimates equipment and installation costs based on home size, system type, and efficiency.

  1. Determine home conditioned square footage
  2. Select system type
  3. Choose efficiency level
  4. Enter specifications
  5. Get multiple quotes from contractors
AI Insights Smart analysis for your HVAC project
❄️
Right-Sizing Analysis
Oversized AC short-cycles and dehumidifies poorly. AI uses Manual J principles to estimate correct tonnage based on your inputs.
💰
SEER Efficiency ROI
16 SEER costs ~15% more than 14 SEER but saves 15-20% annually. AI calculates payback period for each efficiency tier.
🔍
Duct Leakage Impact
20-30% energy lost through leaky ducts. AI estimates potential savings from duct sealing based on your home size.

Calculation Formula

System size = Home size / 400-600 sq ft per ton Equipment = Size x Price per ton Installation = 50-100% of equipment cost

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a new HVAC system cost?
Central AC + furnace: $5,000-$12,000 installed. Heat pump: $4,000-$10,000. Mini-split: $2,000-$8,000.
How long do HVAC systems last?
Central AC: 15-20 years. Furnace: 15-25 years. Heat pump: 10-15 years. Regular maintenance extends life 5+ years.
