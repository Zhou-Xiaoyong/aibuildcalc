How to Use This Calculator

Knowing the weight of your concrete pour is essential for structural planning and truck logistics. Our calculator gives you weight estimates in pounds and tons.

  1. Determine your total concrete volume in cubic yards
  2. Select concrete type (standard, lightweight, heavy)
  3. Enter your volume in the calculator
  4. Check truck weight limits for delivery planning

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a cubic yard of concrete weigh?
Standard concrete weighs about 4,050 pounds per cubic yard. Lightweight concrete weighs about 3,300 lbs/cu yd. Heavy concrete can exceed 4,500 lbs/cu yd.
How much concrete can a standard truck carry?
A standard concrete truck carries 8-10 cubic yards (about 32,000-40,500 lbs). Legal weight limit is 80,000 lbs gross vehicle weight.
